About the Sidemen

History

The sidemen began in October 2013 when a group of friends started playing GTA Online together. Since then, they have grown into one of the most influential YouTubers in the world.

Members (Unordered List)

  • KSI (Olajide Olatunji)
  • Miniminter (Simon Minter)
  • Zerkaa (Josh Bradley)
  • TBJZL (Tobi Brown)
  • Behzinga (Ethan Payne)
  • Vikkstar123 (Vikran Singh Barn)
  • W2S (Harry Lewis)

Milestones (Ordered List)

  1. 2013 - Group officially forms
  2. 2016 - Their book, Sidemen: The Book, became a number one best-seller in the UK
  3. 2020 - They grew their YouTube channel to over 10 milliion subscribers
  4. 2021 - They expanded their business ventures with a subscription service app called Side+
  5. 2022 - Their charity match earned them a Guinness World Record for the most viewers fo a charity sports match live stream on YouTube
  6. 2025 - They sold out the Wembley Stadium in jsut three hours for their charity match

Member Highlights (Definition List)

KSI
KSI is one of the most prominent members, known for his dynamic personality and diverse career as a boxer and musician. He's often seen as a co-leader of the group, and his highly successful ventures outside of YouTube, such as his fights and music career, bring a massive audience to the Sidemen.
Miniminter
Miniminter is celebrated for his quick wit, sarcastic humor, and engaging video content, primarily focused on gaming and vlogs. He is often considered the "glue" of the group, and his long-time friendship with KSI forms the foundation of the Sidemen.
Zerkaa
A key business mind behind many of the Sidemen's ventures like Sidemen Clothing and Side+. He is known for his consistent high-quality gaming content, particularly with FIFA and Call of Duty
TBJZL
TBJZL, also known as Tobjizzle, is praised for his upbeat and charismatic personality. He is a talented creator known for his infectious laugh, energetic presence in group videos, and passion for fashion and music, which he often incorporates into his content.
Behzinga
Behzinga is admired for his candid and often vulnerable vlogs on his fitness journey and mental health. He is a passionate creator known for his energetic and humorous personality, and he has successfully expanded his content into fitness and lifestyle vlogs.
Vikkstar123
Vikkstar123 is a highly skilled and strategic gamer, widely known for his expertise in titles like Minecraft and Call of Duty. He is often highlighted for his calm demeanor and intelligence, playing a key role in the group's collaborative challenges and videos.
W2S
W2S, or Wroetoshaw, is known for his chaotic and unpredictable energy, which brings an element of humor to the Sidemen's content. He is particularly famous for his entertaining FIFA pack opening videos and is often the source of some of the group's funniest moments.
